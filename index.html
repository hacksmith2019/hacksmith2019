ABOUT

HackSmith2019 is Smith's 5th annual hackathon, inviting students from all over the East Coast to spend an amazing 24 hours at Smith College building products that matter. We've been committed to a 50:50 gender ratio since Day 1, and every year we inch closer to disrupting the gender gap in tech! At HackSmith, expect great food, sleepless nights, and too much fun!


FAQ

  • What is a hackathon?
    A hackathon is a 24-hour event where awesome people get together to make cool apps, websites, games, gadgets, etc...
    Can I attend?
    HackSmith is open for everyone! Well, as long as you're 18 and/or a college student. We do encourage curious, innovative, and creative individuals to attend!
    How do I get there?
    We will update later. Please keep tuned!
    I'm new to this...
    This is a great place to learn. We will have mentors and workshops throughout HackSmith to help you learn something new.
    What about travel reimbursement?
    Limited travel reimbursement will be provided to students who are not from the five-college area. Please indicate that you'd like travel reimbursement on your application.
    And teams?
    If you're in need of teammates or ideas for a hack, we will help match you during a team formation session the day of the hackathon.
    Can I sleep overnight?
    We will update later. Please keep tuned!

    What should I bring?
    Your ID, laptop, phone, chargers, and -- if you'd like -- sleeping bags, but most importantly a great hacking spirit!
